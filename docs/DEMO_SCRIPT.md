# Cinematic Demo Script: APEX (3.5 Minutes)
*Optimized for maximum judge excitement and standing ovations.*

## Roles
* **Speaker:** Pitching the vision and explaining the tech.
* **Driver:** Operating the laptop, executing the UI flawlessly.

---

## 0:00 - 0:30 | The Hook (The Problem)
**[Screen: A standard, passive chatbot UI (competitor mock)]**
**Speaker:** "A 2024 McKinsey study found that industrial operators spend 35% of their time searching for information. So, the standard AI hackathon solution is to build a chatbot over PDF manuals. But imagine you are a field technician in a chemical plant. A reactor is overheating, alarms are blaring, and pressure is spiking. You do *not* have the time to pull out an iPad and type: 'Dear AI, what is the SOP for a thermal runaway?'"

**[Screen: Switch suddenly to the APEX Dark-Mode Dashboard. It looks like a SpaceX control interface.]**
**Speaker:** "You don't need a chatbot. You need an Autopilot. Meet APEX: The Causal Decision Intelligence Brain for heavy industry."

## 0:30 - 1:30 | The Incident & Shadow Simulation (The Tech Flex)
**[Screen: Dashboard is green. Driver clicks 'Simulate Incident' hidden button. Red alarms flash. 'CRITICAL: Vibration anomaly detected on Cooling Pump P-202.']**
**Speaker:** "An anomaly just hit our telemetry stream. Instead of waiting for a search query, APEX instantly springs into action. Watch the Causal Map."

**[Screen: The React Flow graph animates. P-202 turns red. A glowing pulse traces the lines from P-202 to Heat Exchanger E-105, and finally to Reactor R-301. A modal pops up: 'BLAST RADIUS CALCULATED: 14 Minutes to Thermal Runaway.']**
**Speaker:** "Under the hood, APEX just built a digital shadow twin, simulated the failure propagation across our NetworkX graph, and cross-referenced the plant P&IDs. It knows the blast radius."

## 1:30 - 2:30 | Decision Intelligence & Runbook Generation (The Wow Factor)
**[Screen: The UI splits. Left side: 3 Mitigation Options with $$ impact. Right side: Empty Runbook.]**
**Speaker:** "APEX's reasoning engine evaluates multiple mitigation paths based on historical incidents and safety codes. We select Path A: Isolate and Engage Backup."

**[Screen: Driver clicks 'Deploy Path A'. The right side instantly populates with a beautiful, 5-step Executable Runbook. Driver toggles to 'Mobile View' to simulate the Field Tech.]**
**Speaker:** "Instead of handing the operator a 50-page PDF, APEX generates a dynamic, executable runbook sent directly to their mobile device. It cites the exact OEM manual page for every step."

## 2:30 - 3:00 | Dynamic Adaptation (The Mind-Blowing Moment)
**[Screen: Driver checks off Step 1. On Step 2 (Close Valve V-12), Driver clicks 'MARK FAILED'. A prompt asks why. Driver types: 'Valve is rusted shut.']**
**Speaker:** "But no plan survives contact with reality. The technician finds the valve is rusted shut. In a normal plant, this means radioing the control room, finding new schematics, and wasting 20 minutes."

**[Screen: APEX UI flashes 'Recalculating Causal Path...'. Step 2 disappears, and Steps 2a and 2b appear: 'Isolate upstream manifold M-05' and 'Bleed pressure at vent V-14'.]**
**Speaker:** "APEX instantly dynamically regenerates the runbook based on the new constraints, routing around the blockage in real-time."

## 3:00 - 3:30 | Compliance & Close (The Business Value)
**[Screen: Driver checks off remaining steps. Clicks 'Resolve Incident'. A beautiful PDF Compliance Audit automatically downloads and opens.]**
**Speaker:** "Once resolved, APEX automatically generates an audit-ready compliance package mapping every action to PESO and Factory Act standards. No paperwork, no lost knowledge."
**Speaker:** "APEX doesn't just retrieve knowledge. It executes it. Thank you."
